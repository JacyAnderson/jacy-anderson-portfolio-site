imAlly.io2017

Sexual Assault Survivor Support Application

About Project

An anonymous, virtual buddy to help connect survivors of sexual assault with appropriate support resources via a conversation-like interface.

Ally.io was initially a passion project of Jacy's after years supporting survivors of sexual assault in a campus environment. For this project Jacy functioned as the Lead Developer/Project Manager, and handled the front end development using Sass, Angular 1, and Gulp.
