About Project

Tennyson street is a growing cultural hub located on Denver's North West side, hosting a wide variety of shops, eateries, events, and breweries.

Shop Tennyson was built through Fireant Studio using Twig/PHP, Sass, JavaScript, Wordpress, Advanced Custom Fields, The Events Calendar, Google Maps API, and much more, to provide businesses around the Berkley neighborhood a new medium to promote themselves and to connect with the community.
